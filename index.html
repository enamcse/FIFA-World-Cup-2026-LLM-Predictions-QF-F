Max 5 points per match, 40 for the tournament. Ties break on mean RPS (ranked probability score), then Brier — both computed from each model's sampled outcome distribution; lower is better.

Prediction protocol

Per model, per match: 1 greedy generation (temperature 0) + 10 sampled (temperature 0.7, fixed seeds). The published prediction is the modal scoreline across all 11 generations; the sample spread doubles as the model's win/draw/loss probability distribution.

Predictions are committed to git before kickoff — the commit timestamp is the tamper-proof receipt. Every raw response, seed, model digest, and job ID is logged in the repo.
